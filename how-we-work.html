Milestone 01

Business Map

Your business plan and marketing strategy become your business map. We understand you know what you want to achieve... you need exact activities and outcomes just like directions on a map.

1

Develop or validate your unique niche market and value proposition

2

Share current market research and competitive analysis within your field of expertise

3

Build or validate your ideal customer avatar based on the exact problem you solve which differentiates you from your competitors

4

Validate the sales channels and how your specialised solution is purchased right now

5

Identify your key metrics for success - activities; result; timeline

Milestone 02

Digital Assets

Your branding is how the world identifies you and your products and services. Consistent repetition builds trust and loyalty and also reduces buyer anxiety for first-time customers. 

1

Utilise brand images and videos to share your business journey with your target audience

2

Develop or validate all business social channels using your brand kit

3

Support the creation of content to articulate your business core values, purpose and brand promise

4

Using posting, vlogging, blogging, digital marketing to share your key messages to reach your audience

5

The exact automation and integration required to onboard new customers, resell to existing customers and can scale for growth

Milestone 03

Website Creation

Your website is your business's online presence where you get to connect, engage and sell to new and existing customers. The more relevant and current your web presence is, the more emotionally invested your visitors will become in your brand and your offerings.

1

Design, Build and Host your website

2

Incorporate the digital functionality required to delight your audience

3

eCommerce facilities, bookings scheduler, automatic messenger to name a few

4

Create a web-to-lead strategy using a specific landing page to generate new leads

5

Create links and add interactivity to engage your visitors

Milestone 04

Measure Impact

SEO and utilising analytics for your business ensure we use all traffic, data and insights to grow your website's organic visitorship. Once optimised, we will increase your credibility, traffic and improve your online visibility and searchability.

1

Install tracking code to gauge high-level metrics and analyse traffic source reports

2

Select and compare data sets and utilise segmentation for more in-depth analysis

3

Support the acquisition of reviews and recommendations to build your social evidence

4

Explore market using Google Trends, Google Keyword Planner, track Adwords and affiliate marketing opportunities

5

Utilise your competitive analysis to identify high performing brands to explore their digital footprint
